Ready to flash

1. Flash firmware

Plug the device in over USB and press Install. Web Serial is used only for the initial flash; configuration happens on the device's SoftAP portal afterwards.

2. First-time provisioning

When flashing finishes, the touchscreen shows a QR code and a SoftAP name like scanner-A4F2 with a password. Scan the QR (or join the AP manually and open http://192.168.4.1) and fill in WiFi + API. The device saves and reboots.

Need to re-open this flow later? Power the device on first, then hold BOOT for ~5 s until the SoftAP setup screen appears. Do not hold BOOT while plugging in — that puts the ESP32-S3 into ROM download mode before firmware runs.
