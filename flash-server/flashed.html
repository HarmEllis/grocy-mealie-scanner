Troubleshooting

I don't see a QR on the device screen
Press the BOOT button on the dev board for ~1 second to re-enter setup mode. The device boots straight into the dashboard once credentials are saved — the setup screen only appears when no credentials exist or BOOT is held. If the screen is still blank, the firmware probably didn't flash cleanly; try the flash flow again.
The popup didn't appear on my phone
Some Android browsers and iOS Lockdown Mode suppress the captive-portal popup. Once your phone is joined to scanner-A1B2, just open a browser and visit http://192.168.4.1 manually — same page, same form.
